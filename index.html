Take your first step into college!





First-Generation: You are the first person in your family to acquire citizenship or attend college. You are still considered first-generation when your siblings attended or is attending college and your parents do not have a college degree.

Low-Income: You are a low income student when your family's income is below 125 percent of the federally established poverty level for their family size. Click HERE for the Federal Definition of Low-Income.

Undocumented: You are an undocumented student when you do not have SSN, permanent residency in the form of a Green Card, DACA/any visas, or when you do have DACA or a visa.


firstStep aims to link students from lower-income households and disadvantaged backgrounds to important and useful educational resources
